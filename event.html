Let's play some chess and chill during next Chess & Chill Aarhus event! It will take place again in Mundhæld bar in Aarhus

Blitz chess is a fast-paced version of chess where each player is given a limited amount of time (usually 3 to 5 minutes) for the entire game. The clock runs down with each move, so players must think and move quickly—perfect for high-speed, high-stakes fun!

Everybody is welcome!
